Introduction

League of Legends is a MOBA, or multiplayer online battle arena, game developed by Riot Games. It was first released on October 27, 2009 and has been one of the most popular, if not the most popular PC game in the world. The game has over ten million daily players with more increasing every year. In order to succeed in this playerbase, one must have thinking skills such as decision making as well as mechanical skills such as reaction time and rapid keyboard actions. Currently, you can sign up for a League of Legends account here!

The game contains hundreds, if not thousands of mechanics that the best players spent years to learn and develop. A large competitive scene with hundreds and thousands of viewers that watch every weekend is built throughout the world. Legendary players such as Faker, xPeke, Perkz have left their legacy in old players' hearts. The game is everchanging and will continue to grow further into the future.

The Riot storywriting department has developed a beautiful world with deep lore and have been continuously creating brand new additions to the worldbuilding. Runeterra is the world they call where the champions of League of Legends to reside in. Iona, Noxus, Demacia, Piltover, Zaun, Mount Targon, and the Shadow Isles are some of the intricate cities and locations that have flesh out stories of their champions. The lore continues to expand as new champions are constantly being developed.
