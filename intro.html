Introduction

League of Legends is a MOBA, or multiplayer online battle arena, game developed by Riot Games. It was first released on October 27, 2009 and has been one of the most popular, if not the most popular PC game in the world. The game has over ten million daily players with more increasing every year. In order to succeed in this playerbase, one must have thinking skills such as decision making as well as mechanical skills such as reaction time and rapid keyboard actions.
