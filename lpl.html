LPL

The LPL stands for League of Legends Pro League and is the competitive series held in China. Matches are held every every day. The format of the LPL is vastly different thant he other regions. The other regions have 10 teams each in their leagues, but the LPL has 17 teams competing in a Single Round Robin. The matches are a best of three.

The current teams in the LPL are:

  • SN - Suning
  • JDG - JD Gaming
  • FPX - FunPlus Phoenix
  • WE - Team WE
