LCS

The LCS stands for League of Legends Championship Series and is the competitive series held in North America. Matches are held every weekend starting Friday after day 1 of LEC is finished. For Sprint Split 2021, the format is a Double Round Robin, where each team plays every other team twice. The matches are a best of one and the top 6 teams qualify for playoffs.

The current teams in the LCS are:

  • TSM - Team Solo Mid
  • C9 - Cloud 9
  • GG - Golden Guardians
  • 100 - 100 Thieves
  • EG - Evil Geniuses
  • CLG - Counter Logic Gaming
  • DIG - Dignitas
  • IMT - Immortals
  • TL - Team Liquid
  • FLY - Fly Quest
