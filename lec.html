LEC

The LEC stands for League of Legends European Championship and is the competitive series held in Europe. Matches are held every weekend starting Friday and is the first western series that begins the weekend. Like the LCS, the format is a Double Round Robin with best of ones. The top 6 teams qualify for playoffs.

The current teams in the LEC are:

  • G2 - G2 Esports
  • FNC - Fnatic
  • AST - Astralis
  • MAD - MAD Lions
  • VIT - Team Vitality
  • S04 - Schalke 04
  • XL - Excel
  • RGE - Rogue
  • MSF - Misfits Gaming
  • SK - SK Gaming
