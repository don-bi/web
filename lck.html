LCK

The LCK stands for League of Legends Champions Korea and is the competitive series held in Korea. Matches are held every weekend starting Thursday. The format of LCK is still a Double Round Robin like the western regions, but matches are best of three instead of best of one. The top 10 teams qualify for playoffs.

The current teams in the LCK are:

  • NS - NS RedForce
  • T1 - T1
  • DK - DWG KIA
  • LSB - Liiv SANDBOX
  • GEN - Gen.G
  • AF - Afreeca Freecs
  • BRO - Fredit BRION
  • KT - KT Rolster
  • DRX - DRX
  • HLE - Hanwha Life
